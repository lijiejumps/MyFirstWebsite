WE THE BOGANS

Just four average bogans tryna make a livin'

Terry Crews - Bassist

Terry was a former bassist player from the band Red Hot Chilli Peppers. Chicago born with a silver spoon, he probably has enough money to settle for the rest of his life. Instead, he decides to join We The Bogans because Australia has the best coffee. When Terry says that he's going to find his lover, he's actually to the gym. Big guy that may seem intimidating, but actually has the softest heart. Terry is really terrified of cockroaches.
