Captain Holt - Drummer

Stage name goes by Captain Holt(born Oct 25 1974). Born and raised in Melbourne City, he's the true blue Bogan amongst the band. If not found drumming, usually spotted collecting spiders or doing the occasional blaze. Special drumming move called the triple slam bang dang.
